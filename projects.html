BiliCam 2013-2014

The goal of this project is to detect the Bilirubin level in new born babies using smartphone camera. A high level of Bilirubin can be life-threatening, while traditional medical device for measuring the level is often very expensive and thus available only in big hospitals. I was officially involved during Sept. 2013 ~ March 2014 but I am no more contributing to the project.

Please refer to the project page for more information:
http://bilicam.com
