Minjoon Seo is a software engineer at NAVER and a Ph.D. candidate in the Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington. He is advised by Hannaneh Hajishirzi, Ali Farhadi, and Oren Etzioni. His research focuses on data-driven learning models for extracting (IE), accessing (QA) and combining (Reasoning) knowledge from natural language. He is a co-organizer of Workshop on Machine Reading for Question Answering (MRQA), which was most recently held at ACL 2018. He also co-organized a tutorial on "Standardized Tests as Benchmarks for Artificial Intelligence" at EMNLP 2018. He has served as a program committee member in ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, ICLR, NIPS, TKDE, COLING, CoNLL, and AAAI. He is the recipient of 2018 AI2 Key Scientific Challenges Award. Previously, he received B.S. in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley.
