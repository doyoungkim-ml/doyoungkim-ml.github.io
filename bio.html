Minjoon Seo is an Engineering & Research Manager at Naver and an incoming Assistant Professor at KAIST. He finished his Ph.D. at the University of Washington under the supervision of Hannaneh Hajishirzi and Ali Farhadi. His study was supported by Facebook Fellowship and AI2 Key Scientific Challenges Award. His research interest is in how the world knowledge can be encoded (e.g. external memory and language model), accessed (e.g. question answering and dialog), and produced (e.g. reasoning). He co-organized MRQA 2018 and 2019 and RepL4NLP 2020.
