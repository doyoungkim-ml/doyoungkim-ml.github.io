Minjoon Seo is a Ph.D. candidate in the Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Hannaneh Hajishirzi and Ali Farhadi, and a research engineer at NAVER (mandatory military service). His research interest has been mostly in the learning model for the extraction of (IE), the access to (QA), and the interplay of (Reasoning) knowledge in unstructured data. He is supported by Facebook Fellowship and AI2 Key Scientific Challenges Award. He is the co-organizer of Workshop on Machine Reading for Question Answering (MRQA) at EMNLP 2019 and ACL 2018 and Workshop on Representation Learning for NLP (RepL4NLP) at ACL 2020.
